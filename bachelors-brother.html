ABOUT THE PROJECT

A Modern Digital Menu & Founder Story for Bachelors Brother.

Bachelors Brother is a rising restaurant that needed a professional online presence to showcase their unique menu and tell the story of the two dedicated employees who built the business from the ground up.

The website was built using Next.js for high performance and deployed on Vercel for seamless reliability, ensuring customers can access the menu instantly on any device.

Overview

The primary goal was to create a fast, mobile-first experience that captures the appetite of customers while building trust through the brand's origin story.

  • Next.js App Router for optimized routing.
  • Fully responsive Digital Menu display.
  • Personalized story section for the founders.
